Main Types of Websites

All the websites that exist in the world can be divided into several basic types. Classification is of course on the functionality of the site and the tasks for which these websites were created. So there are the following basic types of websites

Types and The Difference Between Types of Websites

  • Local Business Website

    Is a small website, up to 10 web pages, which tells about the company, the main type of activity, gives contact numbers and very little information. It fully justifies its name, it is a business card. Nothing online to order or pay it is impossible. This website is focused on one particular small town.

  • National Website

    The second type is a national site it is similar to the previous one but may have a few pages, usually up to 50 pieces, the site provides information about a large number of different possible services and services. There is an opportunity to apply online, rarely pay anything. Such sites are also usually located in one, but already a larger city. For this type of site is already required and very important work on SEO

  • Enterprise Website

    The so-called Enterprise websites are larger in number of pages than the previous types, they require a lot more work on SEO, a lot more work on digital marketing, and social networking because the competition is much more, plus these sites are usually positioned across the whole country and have offices in many cities in the United States, maybe even several countries

  • eCommerce Website

    The last and most difficult type is eCommerce websites, which can sell goods or services; the last type is eCommerce sites, which can sell goods or services across the country, too many countries, or locally to a small town. Usually, at these websites, you can immediately pay for the product/service, order delivery, inquire about availability, and Consult with a representative. These sites are quite complex and competitive and require a lot of hours of SEO, brand management, reputation management, hacking protection, and much more.
